4. **Character portrait area**: A gold-framed circular or shield-shaped portrait of a small gnome archmage with a massive staff, glowing arcane runes floating around him, enormous pointy hat, wise ancient eyes. Heraldic, stylized, not photorealistic.
5. **Stats section**: Three stat values displayed in a clean fantasy UI bar. IMPORTANT: exactly ONE small icon per stat, no duplicates.
